About Mushrad

MUSHRAD is a start-up company under Technology Transfer, Licensing and Commercialisation Centre which is focused on the manufacture of ultrasound gel, a coupling medium in all ultrasound procedures. The company seeks to create a gel that is competitive and can hugely benefit the health care facilities in all ultrasonic diagnostic or therapeutic procedures. Mushrad is set up for growth in all facets of its business by innovation, to allow production of ultrasound gel which is acoustically perfect and of good quality.
